Our Events

Join us for exciting competitions, workshops, and flying sessions!

RC Plane Competition

Get ready for an epic showdown in the skies!

The club's signature event for freshmen, the RC Plane Competition, is a gateway to the world of flight. Under the guidance of experienced club seniors, you'll delve into the fundamentals of aerodynamics, designing and flying your very first airplane!

Event Highlights:
  • 1.5 months of intense competition
  • 125+ participating teams
  • Teams of up to 4 students
  • Hands-on experience in design and construction
  • Expert guidance from seniors

Airshow and UAV Expo

The RC 3D Airshow is an exhilarating display of aeromodelling prowess! Held on the Gymkhana grounds, the event brings together some of the best pilots and aeromodellers to showcase their incredible flying skills.

Featured Attractions:
  • Aerobatic stunts by top Indian RC pilots
  • Electric planes and helicopters
  • FPV drones and gliders
  • Innovative UAV projects
  • Special demonstrations

Workshops & Training

From glider building to hovercraft creation, our workshops offer hands-on experience in various aspects of aeromodelling.

Workshop Series:
  • Glider Workshop for Freshies
  • Hovercraft Workshop
  • DroneZone - Flight Controller Workshop
  • Technical Summer School
  • AE229 Lab Course

Lukkha Flying Sessions

During January and February, we keep the excitement soaring with our weekend Lukkha Flying Sessions. Perfect for those who can't get enough of flying!

Session Features:
  • Regular weekend sessions
  • RC flying practice
  • Aerobatic maneuvers training
  • Payload mechanism experiments
  • Quad flying opportunities
