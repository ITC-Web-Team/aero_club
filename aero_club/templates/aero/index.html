About Us

The Aeromodelling Club at IIT Bombay stands as a beacon for technical innovation and exploration, where the sky is not the limit, but just the beginning. From drones and planes to rockets and hovercrafts, the club is at the forefront of anything that takes flight, drawing enthusiastic participation from over a thousand students across various disciplines.

At the heart of the club's philosophy is a hands-on approach to learning. Members are not just provided with resources but are also immersed in a dynamic peer environment that encourages creativity and innovation. If it can fly, glide, or soar, we're all about making it happen!
